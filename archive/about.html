About

Ben Zenker

I am filled with both creative and technological passions. These stem from my love of math and art, which both began at an early age.

I spent high school juggling these paths. My participation in Project Lead the Way courses and FIRST Robotics displayed my ardent love for math and technology. Simultaneously I taught myself video production for my personal YouTube channel and student produced my high school's morning broadcast.

Entering the Motion Picture Science program at RIT was the best choice I could have made. The program allows me to pursue both my creative and technological passions, spanning a bridge that is so necessary in today's business environment.

My journey through college has only provided growth and guidance towards the directions I am most interested in. I have summarized my passions into six categories, which can be viewed in the circular connection below. Those categories are: Video Production, Video Science, Imaging Science, Software Engineering, Mobile/Web Design, Advertising & Public Relations.
